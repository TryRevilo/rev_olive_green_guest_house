    About us

    OliveGreen Guest House is located next to Komarock Estate, a 25-minute drive from the Nairobi Central Business District. Built in 2005 it incorpiorates some urban charm and our rooms feature well and up to date neccesities. This 4-storied hotel is a beautiful combination of traditional and modern facilities. A 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern day scratch and guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay.

    Our accomodation packages include

    • Single Rooms
    • Double Rooms
    • Family Rooms
    • Meeting Hall (Up to 70 People)
    • Ample FREE Car Park
